# Right View

Right View (_sammā-diṭṭhi_) is seeing reality as it is rather than as it appears.
It is the first factor of the [Wisdom division](index.md#wisdom-pa-ñ-ñ-) and, in
many tellings, the one the others depend on.

## What it covers

Understanding the Four Noble Truths, the nature of suffering, and the principle
of cause and effect (_kamma_).

:::tip Practice
Right View is cultivated, not merely believed — it deepens as the other seven
factors mature.
:::
