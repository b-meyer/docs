# Right Intention

Right Intention (_sammā-saṅkappa_) is the resolve that follows from
[Right View](RightView.md): the turning of the will toward renunciation,
good-will, and harmlessness.

## Three intentions

| Intention    | Turns away from |
| ------------ | --------------- |
| Renunciation | craving         |
| Good-will    | ill-will        |
| Harmlessness | cruelty         |

These are dispositions of the heart, not one-time decisions.
