# Right Speech

Right Speech (_sammā-vācā_) is the first factor of the Ethical Conduct division.
It rests on the intentions cultivated in [Right Intention](RightIntention.md).

## Four guidelines

1. Abstaining from false speech.
2. Abstaining from divisive speech.
3. Abstaining from harsh speech.
4. Abstaining from idle chatter.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The test offered in the early texts: is it true, is it beneficial, is it timely?
